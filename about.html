Our Story

Proudly Zambian

Letta Insurance Brokers Limited is a Zambian company incorporated on 2nd August 2025 with the sole aim of providing broking insurance services and products. Established as a reliable insurance service provider with emphasis on quality management and sound business ethics.

We abide by the professional code of conduct to ensure that clients' and stakeholders' interests are protected. Excellent customer service is at the centre of our offerings.
